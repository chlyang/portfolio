Eternality Sharpener

This design inspires from a poem of 《stray bird》 by Tagore: Let life be beautiful like summer flowers, and death like autumn leaves.Life is short but also fantastic. It just exists, in different forms, and finally reaches the eternality.

The withering of a tree creates a pencil, and in this design, the pencil will present the growing process of a tree.

When we sharp the pencil, the scrap of the pencil will fall slowly like the leaves. I try to integrate into this design the “sad and elapse” feelings of life, reminding people to cherish every details of life.
