与美国Agdia公司的合作项目，他们主要提供植物病毒检测的服务，希望设计一款适用于windows 7 pro平板的应用提供给用户植物病毒检测的服务。在了解清楚他们的植物检测流程之后，我觉得用DNA作为这套设计的主线。

This is a cooperate project with Agdia company who provides the service of virus testing and wishes to develop an application for windows 7 pro tablet for their service. After understanding the main virus testing process, I decided to use DNA as the main element in this design. 


详情页面我提供了1、2两种选择，Agdia希望整体保持浅色色调，所以最后选择了第二个方案。
Between the two choices for detail page, Agdia has chosen the style with lighter color. 

依据DNA的曲线方向，向右滑动开始进行植物检测。
Tracing the direction of DNA curve, the test can be swipe to start.  

在进行完windows app的设计之后，Agdia希望整个产品与app风格适应，所以我同时也帮他们设计了检测产品的外观。

After the design for windows app, Agdia company wanted to keep the industrial product consistent with the application style, so I also developed the industrial product for Agdia.
